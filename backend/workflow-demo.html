Lead intelligence pipeline

Each stage is designed to be auditable before a buyer receives a CSV, PDF report, CRM export, or storefront fulfillment.

1

Ingest

Import public, permitted, or buyer-provided lead sources into the NEXUS workspace.

2

Filter

Keep Tampa Bay targets and remove out-of-scope records before enrichment starts.

3

Validate

Check website, location, category, territory, source notes, and data completeness.

4

Enrich

Add AI summaries, buyer angle, risk notes, and outreach context for the target niche.

5

Score

Rank leads by fit, completeness, source quality, local relevance, and actionability.

6

Publish

Move approved packages to storefront, checkout, CRM export, and delivery review.
