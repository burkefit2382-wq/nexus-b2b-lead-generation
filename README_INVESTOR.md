## Technology & Innovation

### Proprietary Capabilities

**AI-Driven Lead Scoring™**
- Local LLaMA 3.2 engine eliminates third-party AI costs
- Real-time quality assessment (0-100 score)
- Predictive buyer intent modeling
- Automated outreach personalization

**Multi-Vector OSINT Enrichment™**
- 10+ data sources aggregated per lead
- Email breach detection via HaveIBeenPwned
- Domain reputation analysis
- Social footprint correlation
- Phone intelligence and carrier lookup

**Scalable Scraping Architecture**
- Playwright-based engine (headless browser automation)
- Google Maps business extraction
- LinkedIn company intelligence
- Tor routing for anonymous research
- Concurrent processing (10+ pages simultaneously)

### Technology Stack

```
Backend: FastAPI (Python 3.11+) | PostgreSQL/SQLite
AI Engine: LLaMA 3.2 (local) | Sentence Transformers
Scraping: Playwright | BeautifulSoup4 | Tor
Frontend: HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript | Real-time Dashboard
API: RESTful | Auto-generated Documentation
```
