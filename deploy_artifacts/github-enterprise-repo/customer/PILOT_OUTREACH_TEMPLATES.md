# Pilot Outreach Templates

Status: Draft  
Audience: Trusted pilot testers and early customers

## Invitation

Subject: Invitation to test LeadGen Virtual Hub

```text
Hi [Name],

We are preparing a controlled pilot for LeadGen Virtual Hub, a lead intelligence workspace for importing leads, enriching them, scoring priority, and reviewing the best opportunities.

The pilot goal is simple: give you real tasks, watch where the product helps, and find what needs to be fixed before launch.

What we would ask from you:
- Complete one guided test session.
- Import a sample or approved lead dataset.
- Run enrichment and scoring.
- Tell us where the workflow is useful, confusing, slow, or untrustworthy.

Please do not upload sensitive or restricted data unless we have explicitly approved that together.

If you are open to participating, reply with the best time for a 30 to 45 minute session.

Thanks,
[Your Name]
```
