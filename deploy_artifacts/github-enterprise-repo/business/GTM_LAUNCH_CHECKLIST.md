# Go-To-Market Launch Checklist

Status: Draft  
Owner: Business owner  
Applies to: Positioning, launch audience, outreach, sales process, website copy, pilot expansion, and customer feedback

## Launch Positioning

LeadGen Virtual Hub is positioned as a lead intelligence workspace for importing leads, enriching them with OSINT and AI, scoring priority, and helping teams act on the best opportunities.

## Target Launch Audience

| Audience | Fit | Launch Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Trusted pilot users | Best for early validation | Recommended first |
| Small sales/BD teams | Useful after core workflows are proven | Later |
| Agencies/consultants | Useful if exports and multi-client workflows are strong | Later |
| Government-facing buyers | Requires stronger compliance evidence | Later, not first public claim |

## Launch Messaging Guardrails

- Say what the product does today.
- Avoid claiming government-grade, FedRAMP-ready, CMMC-ready, or compliant unless evidence supports it.
- Use "readiness packet" or "security evidence packet" until formal certification exists.
- Explain AI enrichment as decision support, not guaranteed truth.
- Explain OSINT enrichment with source/provenance limits.

| Asset | Needed Before Public Launch | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Website or product page | Clear product promise and sign-up path | TBD |
| Demo script | Shows import, enrichment, scoring, dashboard, export | Draft needed |
| Pricing page or sales CTA | Matches billing behavior | TBD |
| Privacy policy | Reviewed and published | TBD |
| Terms of service | Reviewed and published | TBD |
| Security/trust page | Evidence-backed claims only | TBD |
| Support contact | Visible and staffed | TBD |

## Launch Sequence

1. Finish private pilot setup.
2. Recruit 3 to 10 testers.
3. Run pilot task script.
4. Fix critical/high issues.
5. Decide paid pilot terms.
6. Prepare public claims and trust page.
7. Open to controlled paid pilots.
8. Expand only after reliability, support, and billing are stable.

## Launch Metrics

| Metric | Target |
| --- | --- |
| Pilot activation | 80%+ testers complete first import |
| First value | Tester reaches enriched/scored lead list in one session |
| Critical bugs | 0 open |
| Support load | Manageable by named owner |
| Trust score | Testers understand why a lead score was assigned |
| Conversion signal | At least 2 testers request continued access or paid pilot |
